Green Spaces Landscaping Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Green Spaces Landscaping

Reason: This name represents a company dedicated to creating and maintaining green spaces in any environment, focusing on sustainability and aesthetic appeal.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Green Spaces Landscaping website is to provide an online platform to:

  • Showcase landscaping services offered by the company.
  • Display a portfolio of completed projects.
  • Share blog articles on sustainable gardening and landscaping tips.
  • Provide contact information for inquiries and quotes.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What services does Green Spaces Landscaping offer?

Scenario 2: How can I get a quote for my landscaping project?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #4CAF50 (Green) - Used for headers, navigation bar, and call-to-action buttons.

Secondary Color: #FFEB3B (Yellow) - Used for background accents, highlights, and secondary buttons.

Typography

Heading Font: 'Lora', serif - Used for all headings.

Body Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif - Used for all body text.
